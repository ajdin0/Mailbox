Frequently Asked Questions

How many emails can I send?

You can send as many as you want! We will never force you to a limit of emails that you can send, but the amount of emails that you can send is limited to the plan you are payingThe bigger the plan you have, the more emails you can send!

How much does Mailerboks cost?

Mailerboks has 3 different plans! The cheapest plan starts at $29, and it goes up to $97 for the highest, and $47 for the mid-tier option

How many accounts can I have?

You can have up to 5 accounts with the cheapest option, 10 with the mid tier, and 20 with the highest paid option. We do not want to limit you so you can also invite your team as well!
